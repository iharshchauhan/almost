Who are we and what we serve?

A family of enthic Operating Systems operatable within your browsers anywhere and anytime, with any device

Frequently asked questions

Have something in your mind which is making you wonder??

Does the RGOS family provide actual Operating Systems?

We certainly provide a special one, the web operating system which is a GUI with built-in interactions accessible in your browser. They provide a convenient and integrated platform that enhances the user experience and fulfills the system of an ecosystem, as you can access them with any device having a browser and an internet.

Are there apps & can we store and preview files?

We offer a suite of native apps designed to facilitate a range of daily tasks. Complementing this, we provide a storage system that helps to efficiently store files and open them with their respective apps. These features are designed to create a comprehensive and intuitive user experience, streamlining workflows and boosting productivity.

Does RGOS have user account and authentication?

Indeed! The RGOS user profile is a universal account across all of the members of the RGOS family. It helps the user identify himself with a name, username, pic, and a bio in the world of RGOS! The profile also have authentication which is a universal passcode for all of RGOS protected by CAPTCHA which will assure saftey and privacy for our user.
