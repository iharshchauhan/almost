    About

    RGOS is an open-sourced, web based OS Family concept built with just Vanilla HTML-CSS-JS. RGOS follows a very modern, sleek, and orange themed GUI. Remember, this project and its members are not fully functional operating systems but a concept trying to fulfill the experience of a desktop... Check Wiki for more info...

    Home page ● Trailer ● Repository ● Instagram

    Acknowledgement

    The RGOS Family thanks the fabulous people who contributed to us; The Founder and Developer, Navaneet.J, and the Co-Founder, Shafi.S.K; design and development contributors, Bragadheesh.R, and Kavesh.M.P; and all the other contributors.

    Credits

    The RGOS uses assets and templates provided by other such fascinating providers. They are:

    1. Icons and illustrations provided by: icons8.com, logisstudio - Flaticon, Google fonts and icons, ENIX Icon Pack.
    2. Native Application's templates provided by: Coding Nepal, Minipaint, TinyMCE, xspreadsheet and PDF.js.
    3. 3rd Party applications avaliable in the RGOS Store is credited in their respective installation pages.
    4. Weather widgets by Weatherwidget and tomorrow.io.

      • better animation
      • consistent sidebar

    5. RGOS user dashboard - universal profile centre (All OSs)
